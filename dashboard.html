HOGWARTS UNIVERSITY

Hogwarts University is a renowned institution of magical education located in Scotland. It is the foremost school of witchcraft andwizardry in the wizarding world.

Established over a thousand years ago, Hogwarts has a rich history and a distinguished faculty dedicated to nurturing young witches and wizards in their magical abilities.

The school is divided into four houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Each house has its own unique traits and values, fostering a sense of camaraderie and competition among the students.

Hogwarts offers a comprehensive curriculum covering various magical subjects, including Potions, Charms, Transfiguration, Defense Against the Dark Arts, Herbology, and many more.

The students also engage in extracurricular activities such as Quidditch, a popular wizarding sport played on broomsticks, and the Duelling Club, where they can practice their magical combat skills.
